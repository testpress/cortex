## MODIFIED Requirements

### Requirement: Header — Replace "Back" text with the screen title

On both the Review Analytics landing screen and the Subject-wise Performance screen, the header currently shows a back chevron with a "Back" text label next to it, and the screen title as a large headline below.

The back chevron SHALL be replaced with a standard arrow, and the "Back" text label SHALL be replaced with the screen title inline — shown directly to the right of the back arrow. The large headline below SHALL be removed.

---

### Requirement: "Performance Overview" title — Reduce size

The "Performance Overview" section heading on the landing screen is currently displayed at a size larger than the header title. It SHALL be reduced to a size that feels appropriate as a section heading — visually smaller than the header title above it.

---

### Requirement: "Explore More Details" — Break out of the box

Currently the "Explore More Details" label and its two action cards are all grouped inside a single white box/card container.

The label SHALL be moved outside and above the box, rendered as a standalone section heading.

The two action cards inside the box SHALL each become their own independent card, no longer wrapped together in a shared container. The result is three visually separate elements: the heading text, the first card, and the second card.

---

### Requirement: Subject-wise Performance — Reduce section title sizes

The "Overall Performance" and "Section Performance" headings on the Subject-wise Performance screen are currently too large. Both SHALL be reduced to a size consistent with standard section headings used across the app.

---

### Requirement: Subject-wise Performance — Center the donut chart(s)

The donut chart(s) SHALL use a responsive centering layout: if all charts fit horizontally on the screen, they SHALL be centered. If they exceed the screen width, they SHALL seamlessly overflow into a standard left-aligned horizontal scroll list.
